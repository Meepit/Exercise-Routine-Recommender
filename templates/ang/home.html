Selection of inefficient routines often leads to demotivation as a result of a lack of progress. Strength and hypertrophy exercise routines are well studied and many well regarded routines incorporate the results in order to maximize their efficiencies.

This is a project that aims to help solve the issue of inefficient exercise routine selection by using decision tree classifiers to pick an exercise routine most suited to an individual's circumstances and goals.
